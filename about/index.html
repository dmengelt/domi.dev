About

Domi is a Backend Developer devoted to understand the whole online payments ecosystem. In his current role he leads the Backend Engineering team at Datatrans. Switzerlands largest Payment Service Provider. Domi spends a lot of time trying to convince merchants to leverage the Google Pay APIs in order to make their checkout experience a lot more user friendly. When he’s not working or researching stuff in the payments world, he enjoys watching ice hockey games, playing squash or go for a run.
